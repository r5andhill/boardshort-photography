I'm Rand — I shoot sunrise and sunset, wherever I happen to be.

iPhone and GoPro. No heavy gear, no studio setup. Just showing up before dawn or staying past dark to catch the light doing something it shouldn't.

This is my daily archive of what the sky looks like from wherever I'm standing — and a portfolio of the work I'm most proud of. The ordinary mornings that turn extraordinary. The angles that only exist for thirty seconds. The color you wouldn't believe if you weren't there.
